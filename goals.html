Strength

My strength is connecting narrative, gameplay, and player experience into one clear design direction.

I focus on how mechanics create emotion, and how systems support the feeling I want players to have.

Developing Skill

I am currently developing my ability to design more complex and scalable systems.

I want to improve how I structure mechanics so they remain clear, but can support deeper gameplay and longer player engagement.

Career Path

I aim to become a game designer focused on systems and level design.

I am especially interested in narrative-driven systems and player experience design, particularly in projects with strong emotional or world-building elements.

6–12 Month Goal

In the next 6 to 12 months, I plan to gain practical industry experience through an internship, ideally working alongside experienced designers in a real production environment.

At the same time, I will continue developing my independent projects, including Wonder Tales and Zhuguang, and refine them into stronger portfolio pieces.

I also plan to participate in more game jams to improve my speed, collaboration, and iteration skills.

In parallel, I will continue polishing my portfolio and website to better present my work.
