☁ Project Overview☁ 项目概览

One Inch Away is a cooperative 2D platformer where two players control magnets with the same polarity, meaning they constantly repel each other.《One Inch Away》是一款双人合作 2D 平台游戏，玩家分别操控同极磁铁角色，彼此会持续排斥。

Because they can never get close, the players must use their repulsive force to navigate platforms, manipulate the environment, and solve cooperative puzzles.由于两者无法靠近，玩家必须把排斥力当作移动与解谜工具，完成平台跳跃与环境交互。

The game explores a paradoxical relationship: two characters who cannot touch, yet must rely on each other to move forward.游戏呈现一种悖论关系：两位角色不能相拥，却必须彼此依赖才能前进。

♥ Narrative Premise♥ 叙事前提

Two magnets dream of holding each other. But because their magnetic poles are the same, they are permanently pushed apart.两块磁铁渴望相拥，但因为极性相同，永远会被彼此推开。

A legend tells of a powerful magnet hidden beyond mountains and rivers that can reverse their polarity. Together, the two set out on a journey to find it.传说在群山彼岸有一块强磁体，能够逆转极性，于是他们踏上共同寻找它的旅程。

“We may not be able to hold each other, but we will always be together.”“我们也许无法拥抱彼此，但我们会一直并肩前行。”

★ Core Gameplay★ 核心玩法

The central mechanic is magnetic repulsion between the two players. Because the force is always active, movement becomes a constant negotiation.核心机制是双角色间的磁力排斥。排斥力持续存在，因此每一步移动都需要协商与配合。

Push each other across gaps借排斥力跨越间隙
Launch to higher platforms弹射到更高平台
Adjust movement via repulsion用排斥力微调位移

Magnetic Repulsion磁力排斥

The two characters continuously push each other away. Players must position themselves carefully to use the repulsion as a movement tool.两名角色会持续相互排斥。玩家需要精确站位，把排斥力转化为有效位移工具。

Platform Navigation平台导航

Levels contain obstacles that require coordination: large gaps, moving platforms, and vertical climbing sections.关卡包含需要协作处理的障碍：大跨度缺口、移动平台与垂直攀爬段落。

Key & Portal钥匙与传送门

Collect the Key to activate the exit portal. Once obtained, players can reach the portal to progress together.收集钥匙以激活出口传送门。取得钥匙后，玩家需共同抵达传送门继续前进。

✎ Design Focus & Reflection✎ 设计重点与复盘

Focus:重点： Unlike traditional co-op games, players directly affect each other's movement through physics. This creates challenge and humor. 不同于常规合作游戏，玩家会通过物理机制直接影响彼此位移，从而同时产生挑战与趣味。

Reflection:复盘： Developing a physics mechanic in 42 hours meant focusing on a single strong idea. Simple mechanics with emergent interactions can create deep gameplay. 在 42 小时内做物理玩法，关键是聚焦一个核心创意。简单机制叠加涌现互动，也能形成有深度的体验。
